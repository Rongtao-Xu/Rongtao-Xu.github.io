I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI), working with Professor Xiaodan Liang, Professor Ivan Laptev, and Professor Ian Reid. Prior to this, I was an Assistant Researcher at the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), and a Visiting Scholar at Southern University of Science and Technology (working with Professor Feng Zheng). I received my Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence from the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) in 2024. During my doctoral studies, I was awarded the CAS President’s Scholarship, the National Scholarship, the Beijing Outstanding Graduate Award, the CAS Outstanding Graduate Award,and received Best Paper Nominations at two flagship IEEE conferences. In 2019,I earned a double bachelor’s degree in Mathematics and Computer Science from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), where I won the highest award in the National Mathematical Modeling Competition. Additionally, I interned at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (under the guidance of Professor Xiang Bai) and at Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence/Galbot (under the guidance of Professor He Wang).

My research focuses on intelligent robotics, embodied intelligence, and multimodal large models. I have published over 40 papers in related academic journals and conferences, including 26 first‑author or corresponding‑author papers in top‑tier international journals and conferences such as IEEE TPAMI/TIP/TNNLS/TIM/TMM/TCSVT/TGRS, RSS/ICRA/ICCV/AAAI/MICCAI/ICME (15 in CAS Zone 1, 6 in CCF‑A, 8 in Tsinghua‑A, 3 ESI Highly Cited Papers, and 1 IEEE Transactions cover paper). I have presented multiple oral papers at conferences such as NeurIPS, AAAI, and ICRA. My work has been cited over 600 times on Google Scholar, with a cumulative impact factor exceeding 200. I have also applied for and been granted 2 invention patents. I am an IEEE member, a member of the China Society of Image and Graphics, the China Graphics Society, and the Chinese Society of Stereology. I serve as a reviewer for international journals and conferences including IEEE TPAMI, IEEE TIP, IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TMM, IEEE TCSVT, IEEE TII, Neural Networks, CVPR, NeurIPS, AAAI, and MICCAI.
